FAQ

Questions buyers usually ask before booking a working session.

The goal is simple: make the next step feel clear, low-friction, and operationally realistic for your team.

Which channels can Assistio automate?

Assistio can be configured for WhatsApp, website chat, email, Instagram, and Facebook workflows depending on your plan and rollout scope.

How long does setup usually take?

That depends on your channels, routing logic, and review speed, but the working session is designed to clarify scope quickly so launch can move without unnecessary delay.

Can a human still take over a conversation?

Yes. Assistio is built to support human takeover when nuance, compliance, or commercial judgment requires a real person.

Can Assistio support multiple languages?

Yes. The site and product messaging can be structured for multilingual use, and the rollout can be configured around the languages your business actively serves.

How do demos and lead details move into our workflow?

During rollout we define whether qualified conversations should move to booking, spreadsheets, CRM steps, or a live human handoff based on your operating model.

What happens if our process is more custom than the plans suggest?

Use the contact form or book a demo. We can review a more tailored setup if your channels, handoff rules, or localization needs go beyond a standard package.
